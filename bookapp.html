Physiotherapy


A physiotherapy assessment is the initial meeting between you and the physiotherapist. It is a 60 minute session which includes an interview of your injury, source of discomfort, personal medical history and a treatment plan for rehabilitation. It also includes a treatment with manual therapy, exercise prescription and various modalities as needed.
Follow up treatments are scheduled for 30 minutes or 45 minutes depending on the complexity of the condition and time required, evaluated by the physiotherapist. It is recommended that follow up treatments are booked with the same physiotherapist every session.
